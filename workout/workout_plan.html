How to progress month by month
Wk 1–4
Learn the movements. Focus on form, not weight. Add 1 rep per exercise each session. Build the mind-muscle connection.
Wk 5–8
Start adding 2.5–5 kg to compound lifts when you hit the top rep range. Push harder on isolation work with drop sets.
Wk 9
Deload week. Same exercises, 40% less volume and load. Let your tendons, joints and nervous system recover.
Wk 10+
Start a new cycle at higher weights. Track your lifts every session — if it's not written down, it's not progressive overload.
